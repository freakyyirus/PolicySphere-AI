Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 2, 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our platform, and improve your overall experience. Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information unless you have provided it to us.

How We Use Cookies

PolicySphere AI uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and saved policy scenarios
  • Maintain your session while using the platform
  • Understand platform usage patterns to improve our services
  • Ensure platform security and detect potential threats

Types of Cookies

Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function correctly. These cookies enable core functionality such as session management and security. They cannot be disabled.

Functional Cookies: Remember your settings, preferences, and saved scenarios to provide a personalized experience. These enhance your usage but are not strictly necessary.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the platform. We use this data to improve features, fix issues, and optimize performance. All analytics data is anonymized and aggregated.

User Control

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time:

  • Cookie Banner: When you first visit, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies via the consent banner.
  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings menu.
  • Opt-out: You can clear all stored cookies by clearing your browser data. Note that this may reset your preferences and saved scenarios.

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent the platform from functioning correctly.
